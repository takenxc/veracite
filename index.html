About Veracite

Veracite was founded in 2026 with a simple premise: young adults deserve a news source that respects their intelligence. Most news is either overwhelming or shallow on context. We do neither.

Every article includes 2–3 months of background so you understand why something matters, not just that it happened. Unfamiliar terms are defined inside articles. We cover conflict, politics, science, technology, culture, and sport — because the world doesn't fit into one category.

We have no advertisers. No political affiliation. No agenda beyond accuracy.

Global Summary

The Middle East is at its most volatile in decades. Iran and Israel exchanged direct military strikes in April — the first open acknowledged exchange between the two nations — raising the spectre of a wider regional war. In Gaza, the death toll has passed 50,000 and famine conditions have been confirmed in the north, while ceasefire talks collapsed for the third time this year. Sudan's civil war entered its third year with 10 million displaced — one of the world's worst humanitarian crises, largely ignored by international media.

In geopolitics, the US–China trade war escalated sharply, with tariffs reaching 145% on key goods, fracturing supply chains that took decades to build.
